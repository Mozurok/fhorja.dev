## Known limitations
- Extraction is ripgrep-heuristic: dynamic imports, re-exports, and aliased calls may be missed. The map points; `grep` confirms.
- Language coverage is per-pattern-set (`<languages covered>`); other languages degrade gracefully.
- For `chain:` scope, fidelity depends on whether a parser was present: a `grep-seed (non-authoritative)` chain may miss barrels, default and dynamic imports, and aliases; a faithful chain names the parser used (ADR-0057 D-2).
